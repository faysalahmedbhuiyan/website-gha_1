About This Course


What does happiness mean to you? Throughout history, individuals have been consumed by the quest for happiness and the exploration of a fulfilling life. In our ever-evolving modern world, characterized by increasing complexity and a multitude of stressors, the pursuit of genuine and lasting well-being has become both highly sought after and challenging to attain.


The Positive Psychology and Well-Being online short course offered by the GLOBAL HAPPY ACADEMY (GHA) provides a comprehensive and practical exploration of strategies to enhance personal happiness. Over the course of six weeks, participants will delve into evidence-based approaches rooted in the latest research and well-being theory. This program takes a multidisciplinary perspective, going beyond the realm of positive emotion to present happiness as a holistic and balanced experience. Guided by the GLOBAL HAPPY ACADEMY, individuals will discover practical models for assessing their own well-being, drawing on insights from psychology, psychiatry, neuroscience, and philosophy.

Throughout the course, participants will explore various elements that contribute to well-being, including the concept of 'flow,' mindfulness practices, the role of spirituality and religion, and the impact of group dynamics. By integrating principles from diverse fields, participants will gain a deeper understanding of how these factors positively influence well-being. The course also emphasizes the evaluation and harnessing of individual strengths to improve personal connections with the world.

Upon completion, participants will leave with a personalized approach to maximize their own happiness and a heightened sense of agency over their future well-being, both in their personal and professional lives. This course offers a valuable opportunity to apply insights from psychology, psychiatry, neuroscience, and philosophy to create a more fulfilling and balanced life.



What this course covers


This course empowers learners to instigate meaningful life transformations by drawing insights from various disciplines, such as science, philosophy, literature, and popular culture. It initiates with a deep dive into personal well-being goals, prompting introspection and cultivating a clearer perspective on happiness. Delving into the realm of neuroscience, participants unravel the intricacies of emotion and explore techniques to elevate daily engagement through the optimization of 'flow' experiences. The exploration extends to the profound impact of spirituality, religion, and mindfulness on well-being, encouraging individuals to assess how their strengths can amplify the quality of social connections. Towards the culmination, the course examines the concept of agency as a pivotal tool for maximizing well-being. Learners are guided to craft a personalized approach for future happiness, synthesizing insights accumulated throughout the course.

Who should take this course


This course will benefit anyone who is seeking to use positive psychology and self-reflection to make meaningful life changes in order to be the best person they can be, whether that is at work or at home. Its multidisciplinary design appeals to a wide range of learners, including those seeking strategies for self-improvement, those interested in enhancing happiness in personal or professional settings, and those wanting to balance work and pleasure for themselves or for their team. Lifestyle and healthcare professionals, such as nutritionists, nurses, psychologists, personal trainers, and life coaches will find particular value in the course’s holistic view of wellness and gain practical tools for cultivating practices that promote happiness. Alternatively, those in the corporate space, including HR and wellness managers, team leaders, and senior executives will learn how to introduce well-being into the work environment and apply a culture of positive psychology that builds on the strengths of their employees. Across contexts, individuals interested in achieving happiness will boost their ability to do so.
